	========================================================
	Installation
	========================================================
	1) Connect your Innioasis Y1 through USB and turn on USB storage
	2) Drop the .rockbox folder from this zip into the root of your device
	3) Unmount your Y1
	4) On your Y1, go to Settings -> Theme Settings -> Browse Theme Files
	5) Select NERV
	
	That's all!
